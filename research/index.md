# Research

## Atmospheric Retrievals

I develop and apply atmospheric retrieval techniques to infer physical and chemical properties of planetary atmospheres.

---

## Brown Dwarf Variability

Time-series observations reveal weather patterns and atmospheric heterogeneity in brown dwarfs.

---

## Polarimetry

Polarized light provides additional information about clouds, aerosols, and atmospheric structure.

---

## JWST Science

JWST enables unprecedented studies of cool atmospheres through high-precision spectroscopy.
