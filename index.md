# Welcome

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Hertfordshire.

My research focuses on understanding the atmospheres of exoplanets and brown dwarfs through spectroscopy, atmospheric retrievals, polarimetry, and radiative transfer modelling.

## Research Interests

- Atmospheric retrievals
- Brown dwarf variability
- JWST observations
- Polarized light from planetary atmospheres
- Radiative transfer
- Cloud physics

## Recent Highlights

- JWST/NIRISS observations of W1935
- Retrieval framework development
- Time-variable brown dwarf atmospheres
